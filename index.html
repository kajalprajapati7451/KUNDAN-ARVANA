About The Project

A Home Crafted for the Few

In one of Pune's strongest and most rapidly rising pin codes — Balewadi — we present a landmark that stands in a league of its own. Kundan Arvaana is a rare architectural gem designed for the UHNI who value space, privacy, precision, and timeless elegance.

Rising 33 floors on an exclusive 1-acre parcel, with just 66 ultra-exclusive residences, Arvaana redefines what luxury means in Pune's skyline.
